A loan calculator is a powerful tool for anyone considering a loan. Here's how to use it effectively:

1. Enter Loan Details

Input the loan amount, interest rate, and loan term. These are the key factors that determine your monthly payment.

2. Review Results

The calculator will show your estimated monthly payment, total interest, and total repayment amount.

3. Compare Scenarios

Adjust the loan amount, rate, or term to see how your payments change. This helps you find the best option for your budget.

4. Plan Your Finances

Use the results to plan your budget and ensure you can comfortably afford the loan.

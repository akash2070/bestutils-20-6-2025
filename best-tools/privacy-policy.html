Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to BestUtils. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our free online tools and calculators.

Key Point: BestUtils is designed to work entirely in your browser. Most of our tools process data locally without sending information to our servers.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide and improve our services:

  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics about which tools are used most frequently
  • Technical Data: Browser type, device information, and IP address for security purposes
  • Cookies: Essential cookies for website functionality and optional analytics cookies
  • Feedback: Any feedback or suggestions you voluntarily provide
Important: We do NOT collect or store the data you enter into our calculators and tools. All calculations are performed locally in your browser.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Providing and maintaining our free tools and services
  • Improving user experience and website performance
  • Understanding which tools are most valuable to users
  • Ensuring website security and preventing abuse
  • Responding to user feedback and support requests

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Limited data collection and retention policies
  • Secure hosting infrastructure

5. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for:

  • Analytics: Google Analytics (anonymized data)
  • Hosting: Secure web hosting providers
  • CDN: Content delivery networks for faster loading

These services have their own privacy policies and we encourage you to review them.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access any personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of analytics tracking
  • Disable cookies in your browser settings

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

About Age Calculator

Our free age calculator helps you determine your exact age in years, months, and days from your birth date to today's date. This precise calculation tool is perfect for various purposes including age verification, milestone tracking, and personal record keeping.

The calculator automatically accounts for leap years, varying month lengths, and provides accurate results down to the exact day, making it reliable for official documentation and personal use.

How to Use the Age Calculator

  1. Select Your Birth Date: Click on the date field and choose your birth date from the calendar
  2. Enter Complete Date: Make sure to select the correct day, month, and year
  3. Calculate Age: Click the "Calculate Age" button to get your exact age
  4. View Results: See your age displayed in years, months, and days
  5. Verify Accuracy: Double-check your birth date if the result seems incorrect
  6. Use for Records: Copy or note down the exact age for any documentation needs

Age Calculation Information

Leap Year Accuracy: Our calculator automatically accounts for leap years (February 29th) to ensure precise age calculations.

Time Zone Consideration: The calculation is based on your local date and time zone for maximum accuracy.

Month Length Variations: The calculator considers different month lengths (28, 29, 30, or 31 days) for exact results.

Privacy: Your birth date information is processed locally in your browser and is not stored or transmitted anywhere.

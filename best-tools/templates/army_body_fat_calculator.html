About Army Body Fat Calculator

The Army Body Fat Calculator uses the US Army's official method to calculate body fat percentage. This method is used to determine if military personnel meet the required body composition standards.

How to Measure

  • Height: Stand straight against a wall without shoes
  • Weight: Use a calibrated scale, preferably in the morning
  • Neck: Measure at the narrowest point below the Adam's apple
  • Waist: Measure at the narrowest point of the torso
  • Hip (Females): Measure at the widest point of the hips

Army Body Fat Formula

For Males: Body Fat % = 86.010 × log₁₀(waist - neck) - 70.041 × log₁₀(height) + 36.76

For Females: Body Fat % = 163.205 × log₁₀(waist + hip - neck) - 97.684 × log₁₀(height) - 78.387

Why Body Fat Standards Matter

The US Army maintains body fat standards to ensure soldiers are physically fit and able to perform their duties effectively. These standards help maintain operational readiness and promote overall health and wellness.

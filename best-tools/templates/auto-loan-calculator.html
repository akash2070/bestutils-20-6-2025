About Auto Loan Calculator

Our free auto loan calculator helps you estimate monthly payments and total costs for your vehicle purchase. Calculate payments based on vehicle price, down payment, trade-in value, sales tax, interest rate, and loan term to make informed car buying decisions.

This comprehensive tool factors in all aspects of auto financing including taxes and trade-ins, giving you accurate payment estimates to budget effectively for your next vehicle purchase.

How to Use the Auto Loan Calculator

  1. Enter Vehicle Price: Input the total price of the car you want to buy
  2. Set Down Payment: Enter how much you plan to pay upfront
  3. Add Trade-in Value: Input the value of your current vehicle (if trading in)
  4. Enter Sales Tax Rate: Input your local sales tax percentage
  5. Set Interest Rate: Enter the annual interest rate offered by your lender
  6. Choose Loan Term: Select the loan duration in months or years
  7. Calculate Payment: Click "Calculate" to see your monthly payment and total costs

Auto Loan Tips & Strategies

  • Shop for Rates: Compare rates from banks, credit unions, and dealerships
  • Improve Credit Score: Higher credit scores qualify for better interest rates
  • Larger Down Payment: More money down reduces monthly payments and total interest
  • Shorter Terms: Shorter loans have higher payments but less total interest
  • Pre-approval: Get pre-approved to know your budget before shopping
  • Total Cost Focus: Consider total cost, not just monthly payment
  • Avoid Upside-down Loans: Don't finance more than the car's worth

Auto Loan Considerations

New vs Used: New cars typically offer lower interest rates but depreciate faster than used vehicles.

Loan Term Impact: Longer terms mean lower payments but more total interest paid over the life of the loan.

Additional Costs: Remember to budget for insurance, maintenance, registration, and fuel costs.

Early Payoff: Check if your lender charges prepayment penalties before making extra payments.

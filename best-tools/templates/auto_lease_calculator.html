Key Terms

Money Factor
The lease equivalent of an interest rate. Multiply by 2400 to get APR.
Residual Value
The car's projected value at lease end, expressed as a percentage of MSRP.
Capitalized Cost
The vehicle's selling price for lease purposes (MSRP minus incentives).

Leasing Tips

  • • Higher residual value = lower payments
  • • Negotiate the selling price, not monthly payment
  • • Consider excess mileage fees
  • • Factor in maintenance and insurance costs

About Auto Lease Calculator

Our Auto Lease Calculator helps you estimate monthly lease payments and total lease costs for any vehicle. Whether you're considering a luxury car, SUV, or economy vehicle, our calculator provides accurate estimates based on industry-standard lease calculation formulas.

Leasing can be an attractive option for drivers who want lower monthly payments, newer vehicles with latest technology, and the flexibility to switch cars every few years. Use our calculator to understand the true cost of leasing and make informed financial decisions.

The calculator factors in all major components of a lease: vehicle depreciation, financing costs (money factor), taxes, fees, and your down payment. Get started by entering your vehicle information above and see your estimated monthly payment instantly.

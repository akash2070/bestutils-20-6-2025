About Body Surface Area (BSA)

Body Surface Area (BSA) is a crucial measurement in medicine used for calculating drug dosages, assessing burn injuries, determining cardiac output, and various clinical applications. BSA provides a more accurate basis for medical calculations than weight alone.

Our calculator uses four validated formulas:

  • • DuBois Formula: Most widely used, developed in 1916
  • • Mosteller Formula: Simpler calculation, commonly used in oncology
  • • Haycock Formula: Accurate for children and adults
  • • Gehan & George: Particularly accurate for pediatric patients

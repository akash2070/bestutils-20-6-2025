Understanding Body Types (Somatotypes)

Body type classification, or somatotyping, was developed by psychologist William Sheldon in the 1940s. This system categorizes human physiques into three main types based on bone structure, muscle mass distribution, and fat storage patterns.

🥖

Ectomorph

Naturally thin, fast metabolism, difficulty gaining weight, long limbs, narrow frame

💪

Mesomorph

Naturally muscular, athletic build, gains muscle easily, broad shoulders, efficient metabolism

🍎

Endomorph

Naturally stocky, slower metabolism, gains weight easily, wider hips, rounder physique

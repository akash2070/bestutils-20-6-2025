About Calorie Calculator

Our free calorie calculator determines your daily caloric needs based on your BMR (Basal Metabolic Rate) and activity level. Calculate both your BMR - the calories needed for basic body functions at rest - and your TDEE (Total Daily Energy Expenditure) for weight management and fitness goals.

This tool uses the scientifically-validated Mifflin-St Jeor equation and activity multipliers to provide accurate calorie estimates for weight loss, weight gain, or maintenance planning.

How to Use the Calorie Calculator

  1. Enter Your Age: Input your current age in years
  2. Select Gender: Choose male or female (affects metabolic calculations)
  3. Choose Units: Select metric (cm, kg) or imperial (ft/in, lbs)
  4. Enter Height: Input your height in the selected unit system
  5. Enter Weight: Input your current weight in the selected unit system
  6. Select Activity Level: Choose your typical daily activity level
  7. Calculate Calories: Click "Calculate Calories" to get BMR and TDEE
  8. Use Results: Apply calorie targets for your specific goals

Calorie Calculator Tips & Applications

  • Weight Loss: Eat 300-500 calories below your TDEE for gradual weight loss
  • Weight Gain: Eat 300-500 calories above your TDEE for healthy weight gain
  • Maintenance: Eat at your TDEE level to maintain current weight
  • Activity Accuracy: Be honest about your activity level for accurate results
  • Regular Updates: Recalculate as your weight and activity level change
  • Quality Matters: Focus on nutrient-dense foods, not just calorie counting
  • Professional Guidance: Consult nutritionists for personalized meal planning

Understanding Your Results

BMR (Basal Metabolic Rate): Calories needed for basic body functions like breathing, circulation, and cell production at complete rest.

TDEE (Total Daily Energy Expenditure): Total calories burned including BMR plus physical activity and exercise.

Activity Levels: Sedentary (desk job), Lightly Active (1-3 days exercise), Moderately Active (3-5 days), Very Active (6-7 days), Extra Active (intense daily exercise).

Accuracy Note: Individual metabolism can vary ±10-15% from calculated values. Monitor your progress and adjust accordingly.

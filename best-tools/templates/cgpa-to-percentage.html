Understanding CGPA and Percentage

CGPA (Cumulative Grade Point Average): A grading system that calculates the average of grade points obtained in all subjects over all semesters.

Percentage System: Traditional grading system showing marks out of 100, commonly used for comparison and applications.

Conversion Variations: Different educational boards and universities may use different conversion formulas based on their grading policies.

Important Note: This converter provides estimates. Always use your institution's official conversion method for formal applications and documentation.

How to Use the Date Calculator

  1. Choose Calculation Type: Select "Difference Between Dates" or "Add/Subtract Time"
  2. For Date Difference: Enter start date and end date to find the time between them
  3. For Add/Subtract: Enter base date, choose add/subtract, enter value and time unit
  4. Select Time Units: Choose days, weeks, months, or years for calculations
  5. Calculate: Click "Calculate" to get precise results
  6. View Results: See detailed breakdown of years, months, days, or resulting date

Date Calculation Information

Leap Year Handling: The calculator automatically accounts for leap years in all calculations.

Month Length Variations: Calculations consider different month lengths (28-31 days) for accuracy.

Time Zone Neutral: All calculations are based on calendar dates without time zone considerations.

Business vs Calendar Days: Results show calendar days; consider excluding weekends/holidays for business calculations.

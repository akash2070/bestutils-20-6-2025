Word Density Analysis Tips

  • SEO Optimization: Identify keyword density for search engine optimization
  • Writing Quality: Check for overused words that may weaken your writing
  • Lexical Diversity: Higher lexical density indicates more varied vocabulary
  • Content Analysis: Understand main themes and topics in your text
  • Academic Writing: Ensure appropriate vocabulary complexity for academic papers
  • Editing Tool: Identify repetitive words that need synonyms or variation
  • Keyword Research: Analyze competitor content for keyword usage patterns

Understanding Word Density Metrics

Total Words: The complete count of all words in your text, including repetitions.

Unique Words: The number of distinct words used, showing vocabulary diversity.

Lexical Density: Percentage of unique words vs total words - higher values indicate richer vocabulary.

Word Frequency: Shows how often each word appears, helping identify overused terms and main themes.

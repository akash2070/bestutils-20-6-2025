About Fuel Cost Calculator

Our fuel cost calculator is a comprehensive tool designed to help you estimate the total gas expenses for any trip. Whether you're planning a vacation, business trip, or daily commute, this calculator provides accurate cost projections based on your vehicle's fuel efficiency, current gas prices, and trip distance.

Key Features

  • Real-time cost calculation
  • Round trip option
  • Detailed cost breakdown
  • Cost per mile analysis
  • Mobile-responsive design

Perfect For

  • Vacation planning
  • Business travel budgets
  • Road trip expenses
  • Daily commute costs
  • Vehicle comparison

How to Use the Fuel Cost Calculator

Step-by-Step Guide

1

Enter Trip Distance

Input the total miles you plan to drive for your trip.

2

Add Vehicle MPG

Enter your vehicle's fuel efficiency in miles per gallon.

3

Input Gas Price

Enter the current price per gallon at your local gas station.

4

Calculate Cost

Click "Calculate Fuel Cost" to get your trip's total gas expense.

Pro Tips

  • Check current gas prices using apps like GasBuddy
  • Use your vehicle's actual MPG, not EPA estimates
  • Consider highway vs city driving differences
  • Add 10-15% buffer for unexpected detours
  • Compare costs with different vehicles before buying

Formula Used

Fuel Cost = (Distance ÷ MPG) × Gas Price per Gallon

* Distance is doubled for round trips

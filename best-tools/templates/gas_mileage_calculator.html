About Gas Mileage Calculator

Our gas mileage calculator is a free, easy-to-use tool that helps you determine your vehicle's fuel efficiency in miles per gallon (MPG). Whether you're tracking your car's performance, comparing different vehicles, or trying to optimize your driving habits, this calculator provides accurate results instantly.

Key Features

  • Fuel efficiency rating
  • Mobile-friendly design

How to Use the Gas Mileage Calculator

Step-by-Step Instructions

1

Record Your Odometer

Note your vehicle's odometer reading before filling up with gas.

2

Fill Your Tank

Fill your gas tank completely and note the amount of gallons used.

3

Drive and Record

After driving, record your new odometer reading and calculate miles driven.

4

Calculate MPG

Enter your miles driven and gallons used, then click "Calculate MPG".

Tips for Accurate Results

  • Always fill your tank completely for accurate measurements
  • Use the same gas station for consistent measurements
  • Track multiple fill-ups for better average MPG
  • Consider different driving conditions (city vs highway)

About This Tool

This Golf Handicap Calculator uses the official USGA handicap system to determine your handicap index. It calculates score differentials and applies the proper averaging formula based on the number of scores submitted.

Formula: Score Differential = (Score - Course Rating) × 113 / Slope Rating

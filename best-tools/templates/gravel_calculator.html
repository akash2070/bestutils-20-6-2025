About Our Gravel Calculator

Our professional gravel calculator helps you determine the exact amount of gravel needed for any project, whether you're building a driveway, creating a landscaping feature, or working on a construction project.

What is Gravel?

Gravel is a collection of small, rounded stones typically used in construction and landscaping. It's an essential material for driveways, pathways, drainage systems, and decorative landscaping. Different types of gravel serve different purposes and have varying densities, which affects how much you'll need for your project.

Common Gravel Types & Uses

🌟 Pea Gravel

Small, rounded stones perfect for walkways, landscaping, and decorative applications. Easy to walk on and excellent drainage.

🏗️ Crushed Stone

Angular stones that compact well, ideal for driveways, roads, and as a base material under concrete or pavers.

🌊 River Rock

Smooth, naturally rounded stones perfect for decorative landscaping, water features, and erosion control.

🏔️ Limestone

Durable and versatile, great for driveways, pathways, and drainage applications. Naturally occurring and cost-effective.

Tips for Success

✅ Measure Accurately: Use a measuring tape for precise dimensions. Round up to the nearest half-foot for safety.

✅ Consider Compaction: Gravel will settle and compact over time, so the waste factor accounts for this.

✅ Check Local Codes: Some areas have requirements for driveway materials and thickness.

✅ Plan for Delivery: Ensure you have access for delivery trucks and a suitable location to dump the gravel.

✅ Prepare the Base: Level and compact the ground before adding gravel for best results.

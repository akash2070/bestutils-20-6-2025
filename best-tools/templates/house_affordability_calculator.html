About House Affordability Calculator

Our house affordability calculator helps you determine how much house you can realistically afford based on your income, existing debts, down payment, and loan terms. This comprehensive tool considers all major factors that lenders evaluate when approving mortgages, giving you a realistic picture of your home buying budget.

Key Factors in Home Affordability:

  • Debt-to-Income Ratio (DTI): The percentage of your gross monthly income that goes toward debt payments
  • Down Payment: The upfront cash payment that reduces your loan amount
  • Interest Rate: The annual percentage rate charged on your mortgage loan
  • Property Taxes: Annual taxes based on your home's assessed value
  • Home Insurance: Required insurance to protect your property
  • PMI (Private Mortgage Insurance): Required if down payment is less than 20%

DTI Ratio Guidelines:

  • 28% (Conservative): Recommended for financial stability
  • 31% (Moderate): Balanced approach for most buyers
  • 36% (Standard): Commonly accepted by most lenders
  • 43% (Maximum FHA): Highest ratio for FHA loans

What's Included in Monthly Payment:

  • Principal and Interest (P&I)
  • Property Taxes
  • Homeowners Insurance
  • Private Mortgage Insurance (PMI) if applicable
  • HOA fees (if applicable)

💡 Home Buying Tips

📊 Improve Your DTI

Pay down existing debts before applying for a mortgage to qualify for a larger loan amount.

💰 Save for Down Payment

A 20% down payment eliminates PMI and reduces your monthly payment significantly.

🏦 Shop for Rates

Compare rates from multiple lenders - even 0.25% can save thousands over the loan term.

📋 Get Pre-approved

Pre-approval gives you a competitive edge and shows sellers you're a serious buyer.

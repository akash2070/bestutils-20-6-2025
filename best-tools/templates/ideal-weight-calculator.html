How to Use the Ideal Weight Calculator

  1. Select Gender: Choose male or female (affects calculation formulas)
  2. Choose Units: Select metric (cm) or imperial (ft/in) for height input
  3. Enter Height: Input your height in the selected unit system
  4. Calculate: Click "Calculate Ideal Weight" to see results from multiple formulas
  5. Review Results: Compare ideal weights from Devine, Robinson, Miller, and Hamwi formulas
  6. Clear: Use "Clear" button to reset all fields for new calculations

Ideal Weight Tips & Health Information

  • Multiple Formulas: Different formulas provide a range - your ideal weight may fall anywhere within this range
  • Individual Variation: Ideal weight varies based on body composition, muscle mass, and bone density
  • Health Goals: Use as a general guideline for setting realistic weight management goals
  • Body Composition: Athletes may weigh more due to muscle mass while still being healthy
  • Age Considerations: Ideal weight ranges may vary slightly with age and life stage
  • Professional Guidance: Consult healthcare providers for personalized weight recommendations
  • Holistic Health: Focus on overall health, not just reaching a specific number

Understanding the Formulas

Devine Formula: Most commonly used in medical settings, developed for drug dosage calculations.

Robinson Formula: Modification of Devine formula, often used in clinical practice.

Miller Formula: Another clinical variation providing slightly different weight estimates.

Hamwi Formula: Originally developed for diabetic patients, widely used in nutrition counseling.

Important Note: These are estimates for general guidance. Individual health needs may vary significantly.

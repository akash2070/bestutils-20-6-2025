Understanding IRAs: Traditional vs Roth

Traditional IRA

  • ✓Tax-deductible contributions
  • ✓Immediate tax benefits
  • ✓Tax-deferred growth
  • ✗Taxed on withdrawals
  • ✗Required minimum distributions at 73

Best for: Those expecting to be in a lower tax bracket in retirement or wanting immediate tax deductions.

Roth IRA

  • ✓Tax-free withdrawals in retirement
  • ✓No required minimum distributions
  • ✓Tax-free growth
  • ✗No immediate tax deduction
  • ✗Income limits for eligibility

Best for: Younger investors, those expecting higher tax rates in retirement, or wanting tax-free legacy planning.

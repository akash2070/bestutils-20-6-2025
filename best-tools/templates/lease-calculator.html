About the Lease Calculator

What is a Lease?

A lease is a contractual agreement where you pay to use an asset (like a car or equipment) for a specific period without owning it. At the end of the lease term, you typically return the asset or have the option to purchase it.

Key Components

  • Capitalized Cost: The vehicle's price or negotiated amount
  • Residual Value: Estimated value at lease end
  • Money Factor: Interest rate for the lease
  • Lease Term: Duration of the lease agreement

Benefits of Leasing

  • Lower monthly payments compared to buying
  • Access to newer models with latest features
  • Warranty coverage throughout lease term
  • No resale hassles
  • Potential tax advantages for businesses

Considerations

  • Mileage restrictions and excess charges
  • Wear and tear fees
  • No ownership equity
  • Early termination penalties

About Long Division Calculator

Long division is a method for dividing large numbers by breaking the division process into smaller, manageable steps. This calculator shows you the complete process with detailed explanations at each step.

Our long division calculator helps you understand:

  • Quotient: The result of the division
  • Remainder: What's left over after division
  • Step-by-step process: How to arrive at the answer
  • Verification: Checking your answer by multiplication

Perfect for students learning division, parents helping with homework, or anyone needing to verify division calculations.

How to Use the Long Division Calculator

Step-by-Step Instructions:

  1. Enter the dividend (the number you want to divide)
  2. Enter the divisor (the number you're dividing by)
  3. Click "Calculate Long Division" to see the solution
  4. Review the quotient, remainder, and step-by-step working
  5. Use the verification to check your answer

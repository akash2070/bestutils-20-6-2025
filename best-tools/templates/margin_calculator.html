About Margin Calculator

What is Profit Margin?

Profit margin is a financial metric that measures the percentage of profit earned on sales. It indicates how much profit a company makes for every dollar of revenue generated.

Types of Margins:

  • Gross Margin: Revenue minus cost of goods sold
  • Net Margin: Final profit after all expenses
  • Markup: Percentage added to cost price

Formulas Used

Profit Margin %:
((Selling Price - Cost Price) / Selling Price) × 100
Markup %:
((Selling Price - Cost Price) / Cost Price) × 100
Profit Amount:
Selling Price - Cost Price

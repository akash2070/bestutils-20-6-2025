About Mass Calculator

Our Mass Calculator is a versatile online tool designed to help you convert between different units of mass and weight quickly and accurately. Whether you're a student, professional, or just need to convert cooking measurements, this calculator handles all common mass units with precision.

The calculator supports conversions between metric units (grams, kilograms, milligrams, metric tons) and imperial units (pounds, ounces, stones, US tons), making it perfect for international use or when working with different measurement systems.

Supported Units

Metric Units:

  • • Milligram (mg)
  • • Gram (g)
  • • Kilogram (kg)
  • • Metric Ton

Imperial Units:

  • • Ounce (oz)
  • • Pound (lb)
  • • Stone
  • • US Ton

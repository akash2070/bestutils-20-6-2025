About This Calculator

Our Mean Median Mode Range Calculator is a comprehensive statistical tool that helps you analyze numerical data sets quickly and accurately. Whether you're a student, researcher, or professional, this calculator provides essential statistical measures that describe the central tendency and variability of your data.

What Each Measure Tells You:

Mean (Average)

The arithmetic average of all values. It's calculated by adding all numbers and dividing by the count. Best represents typical value when data is normally distributed.

Median

The middle value when data is arranged in order. Less affected by extreme values (outliers) than the mean. Better for skewed distributions.

Mode

The most frequently occurring value(s). There can be one mode, multiple modes, or no mode. Useful for categorical data and finding common values.

Range

The difference between the largest and smallest values. Provides a simple measure of variability or spread in your data set.

How to Use This Calculator

1

Enter Your Data

Type your numbers in the text area. You can separate them with commas, spaces, or put each number on a new line. Both integers and decimal numbers are supported.

2

Click Calculate

Press the "Calculate Statistics" button to instantly compute all statistical measures for your data set.

3

View Results

Your results will appear below with detailed explanations. You'll see the mean, median, mode, range, plus additional information like count, sum, and sorted data.

4

Clear and Restart

Use the "Clear All" button to reset the calculator and start with new data. Perfect for analyzing multiple data sets.

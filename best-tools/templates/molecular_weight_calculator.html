What is Molecular Weight?

Molecular weight (also called molar mass) is the mass of one mole of a substance, expressed in grams per mole (g/mol). It's calculated by summing the atomic weights of all atoms in a molecule, multiplied by their respective quantities in the chemical formula.

Complex compounds: K4[Fe(CN)6], [Cu(NH3)4]SO4

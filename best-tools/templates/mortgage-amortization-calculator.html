About the Mortgage Amortization Calculator

Our mortgage amortization calculator is a comprehensive tool that helps you understand the complete financial picture of your home loan. Unlike simple payment calculators, this tool provides a detailed breakdown of every payment throughout your loan term, showing exactly how much goes toward principal and interest each month.

What is Mortgage Amortization?

Mortgage amortization is the process of paying off your home loan through regular monthly payments over a specified period. Each payment consists of two parts: principal (which reduces your loan balance) and interest (the cost of borrowing). Early in your loan term, more of your payment goes toward interest, while later payments contribute more toward the principal balance.

🔬 About Ovulation Tracking

Ovulation is the process where your ovaries release a mature egg, typically occurring around day 14 of a 28-day menstrual cycle. Understanding your ovulation timing is crucial for pregnancy planning and reproductive health.

Ovulation Window

⚠️ Important Note

This calculator provides estimates based on average cycle patterns. For precise tracking, consider using additional methods like basal body temperature monitoring, ovulation predictor kits, or consulting with a healthcare provider. Individual cycles can vary, and this tool should not replace professional medical advice.

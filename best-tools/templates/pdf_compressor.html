❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How much can I compress my PDF file?
Compression rates vary depending on your PDF content. Text-heavy documents may see 20-50% reduction, while image-heavy PDFs can be compressed by 70-90% without significant quality loss.
Is my PDF file safe and secure?
Absolutely! All compression happens locally in your browser. Your files never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy and security. We don't store or access your documents.
What's the maximum file size I can compress?
You can compress PDF files up to 100MB. For larger files, consider splitting them into smaller documents or using our batch compression feature.
Will compression affect document quality?
Our smart compression algorithms are designed to maintain document readability and professional appearance. Text remains crisp, and images are optimized without noticeable quality loss.
Can I compress password-protected PDFs?
Currently, password-protected PDFs need to be unlocked before compression. You can re-add password protection after compression if needed.

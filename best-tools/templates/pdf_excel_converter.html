Frequently Asked Questions

What file formats are supported for conversion?
We support PDF files for input and can convert them to Excel (.xlsx) format. For reverse conversion, we accept Excel files (.xls, .xlsx) and convert them to PDF. Our tool handles both text-based and image-based PDFs.
Is there a file size limit for uploads?
Yes, we support files up to 50MB in size. This generous limit accommodates most business documents, reports, and large datasets while ensuring optimal performance.
How accurate is the PDF to Excel conversion?
Our converter uses advanced parsing algorithms to maintain high accuracy. Tables, formatting, and data structure are preserved as much as possible. For complex layouts, accuracy typically ranges from 85-95%.
Is my sensitive data safe and secure?
Absolutely! All processing happens locally in your browser using client-side JavaScript. No files are uploaded to our servers, ensuring complete privacy and data security for confidential business documents.
Do I need to install any software or plugins?
No installation required! Our converter works entirely in your web browser. It's compatible with Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. Simply upload your file and convert instantly.
Can I convert password-protected PDF files?
Currently, our tool works best with non-password-protected PDFs. For password-protected files, please remove the password protection first, then upload for conversion.
How many files can I convert per day?
There are no daily limits! You can convert as many files as you need, completely free. Our tool is designed for both individual users and businesses with high-volume conversion needs.
What if my PDF contains charts or images?
Our converter focuses on extracting tabular data and text. Charts and images are not converted but the underlying data tables are extracted when possible. For complex visual elements, manual review may be needed.

About the Percent Off Calculator

Our percent off calculator is a powerful tool designed to help you quickly calculate discounts, sale prices, and potential savings. Whether you're shopping online, comparing deals, or planning your budget, this calculator provides instant and accurate results.

The calculator works by taking the original price and discount percentage to compute the final sale price and the amount you'll save. It's perfect for shoppers, retailers, and anyone who needs to calculate percentage discounts quickly and accurately.

How to Use the Percent Off Calculator

1

Enter Original Price

Input the original price of the item before the discount. You can enter any positive number with up to two decimal places.

2

Enter Discount Percentage

Input the discount percentage (0-100%). For example, enter "25" for a 25% discount.

3

Click Calculate

Press the "Calculate Discount" button to instantly see your results, including the sale price and total savings.

4

View Results

The calculator will display the final sale price, the amount you save, and a summary of your calculation.

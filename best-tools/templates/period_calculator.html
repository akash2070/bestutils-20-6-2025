About the Period Calculator

Our period calculator is a comprehensive tool designed to help women track their menstrual cycle and predict important dates for reproductive health. By entering your last period date and cycle information, you can predict your next period, ovulation date, and fertile window.

This calculator uses standard calculations based on average cycle lengths and is designed for educational purposes. The menstrual cycle typically ranges from 21-35 days, with the average being 28 days. Ovulation usually occurs around day 14 of a 28-day cycle.

Disclaimer: This calculator provides estimates based on average cycles. Individual cycles can vary. For medical advice or concerns about your menstrual cycle, please consult with a healthcare professional.

Understanding Your Menstrual Cycle

Menstrual Phase

The period phase when the uterine lining sheds. Typically lasts 3-7 days.

Ovulation

When an egg is released from the ovary. Usually occurs around day 14 of a 28-day cycle.

Fertile Window

The days when pregnancy is most likely. Includes 5 days before ovulation and ovulation day.

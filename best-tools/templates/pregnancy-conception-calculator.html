How to Use the Pregnancy Conception Calculator

  1. Choose Calculation Method: Select "Last Menstrual Period (LMP)" or "Estimated Due Date"
  2. Enter Date: Input your LMP date or estimated due date using the date picker
  3. Calculate: Click "Calculate Conception Date" to get your estimated conception date
  4. Review Results: See your estimated conception date displayed clearly
  5. Clear for New Calculation: Use "Clear" to reset and try different dates

Conception & Pregnancy Tips

  • LMP Method: Most common method - conception typically occurs 14 days after LMP
  • Due Date Method: Works backward from due date - conception is about 266 days before
  • Ovulation Window: Conception usually happens during a 6-day window around ovulation
  • Cycle Variations: Individual cycles may vary from the standard 28-day assumption
  • Accuracy: These are estimates - actual conception can vary by several days
  • Medical Confirmation: Ultrasounds provide more accurate dating, especially early in pregnancy
  • Planning Purposes: Use for general planning and understanding pregnancy timeline

Understanding Conception Dating

Conception Window: Sperm can survive up to 5 days, and eggs are viable for 12-24 hours, creating a conception window.

Calculation Methods: LMP method assumes 28-day cycles with ovulation on day 14. Due date method works backward 266 days.

Individual Variation: Actual conception dates may vary based on cycle length, ovulation timing, and individual factors.

Medical Disclaimer: This calculator provides estimates only. Consult healthcare providers for accurate pregnancy dating and care.

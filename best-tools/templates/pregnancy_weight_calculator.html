About the Pregnancy Weight Gain Calculator

Our pregnancy weight gain calculator helps expectant mothers track healthy weight gain throughout pregnancy based on pre-pregnancy BMI and medical guidelines from the Institute of Medicine (IOM) and American College of Obstetricians and Gynecologists (ACOG).

How to Use This Calculator

  1. Enter your pre-pregnancy weight - This is your weight before conception
  2. Input your height - Choose between feet/inches or centimeters
  3. Specify current week of pregnancy - Enter your current gestational week
  4. Add current weight (optional) - To track your progress
  5. Select pregnancy type - Single baby, twins, or multiples
  6. Click "Calculate Weight Gain" - Get personalized recommendations

Understanding Your Results

  • Pre-pregnancy BMI: Categorizes your weight status before pregnancy
  • Total Recommended Gain: Evidence-based weight gain range for your entire pregnancy
  • Weekly Gain: Recommended weekly weight gain for second and third trimesters
  • Current Progress: How your current weight gain compares to recommendations

Weight Gain Guidelines by BMI

  • Underweight (BMI < 18.5): 28-40 lbs total gain
  • Normal weight (BMI 18.5-24.9): 25-35 lbs total gain
  • Overweight (BMI 25.0-29.9): 15-25 lbs total gain
  • Obese (BMI ≥ 30.0): 11-20 lbs total gain

Important Note

This calculator provides general guidelines based on medical recommendations. Always consult with your healthcare provider for personalized advice about weight gain during pregnancy, especially if you have specific health conditions or concerns.

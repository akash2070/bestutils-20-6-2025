QR Code Tips & Best Practices

  • URL Formatting: Include "https://" for web links to ensure proper scanning
  • Content Length: Shorter content creates simpler, more scannable QR codes
  • Error Correction: High error correction level allows scanning even if partially damaged
  • Size Considerations: Larger QR codes are easier to scan from distance
  • Contrast: Ensure good contrast between dark and light areas when printing
  • Testing: Always test QR codes with multiple devices before final use
  • Contact Info: Use vCard format for contact information QR codes

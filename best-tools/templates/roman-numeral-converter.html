About the Roman Numeral Converter

The **Roman Numeral Converter** is a simple yet powerful tool that allows you to instantly convert between traditional Arabic numbers (like 1, 2, 3) and Roman numerals (like I, II, III). Whether you're a student learning about ancient numbering systems, a history enthusiast, or just need a quick conversion, this tool has you covered.

Roman numerals were the numeric system used by the ancient Romans and are still used today for clock faces, book chapters, royal names, and more.

How to Use the Roman Numeral Converter

  • Convert Arabic to Roman: Enter an Arabic number (between 1 and 3999) into the "Arabic Number" field and click "Convert to Roman".
  • Convert Roman to Arabic: Enter a valid Roman numeral into the "Roman Numeral" field and click "Convert to Arabic".
  • The result will be displayed below the input fields.

Note: Valid Roman numerals typically represent numbers from 1 to 3999 (although higher numbers can be represented with bars, this converter focuses on the common range).

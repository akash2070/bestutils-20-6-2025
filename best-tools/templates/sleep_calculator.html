About Sleep Calculator

Our Sleep Calculator helps you determine the optimal bedtime and wake time based on natural sleep cycles. Each sleep cycle lasts approximately 90 minutes, and waking up at the end of a cycle helps you feel more refreshed.

The calculator accounts for the average 15 minutes it takes to fall asleep and provides multiple wake-up time options based on completing 4, 5, or 6 sleep cycles.

How to Use

For Wake Time Calculator:

  1. Enter your desired bedtime
  2. Click "Calculate Wake Times"
  3. Choose from suggested wake times

For Bedtime Calculator:

  1. Enter your required wake time
  2. Click "Calculate Bedtimes"
  3. Choose from suggested bedtimes

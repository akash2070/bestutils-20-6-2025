📊 About Speed Testing

  • Download Speed: How fast you can receive data from the internet
  • Upload Speed: How fast you can send data to the internet
  • Ping/Latency: Time it takes for data to travel to a server and back
  • Good Download: 25+ Mbps for streaming, 100+ Mbps for multiple devices
  • Good Upload: 3+ Mbps for video calls, 10+ Mbps for content creation
  • Good Ping: Under 50ms for gaming, under 100ms for general use

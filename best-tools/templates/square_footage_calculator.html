About Square Footage Calculator

Our Square Footage Calculator is a comprehensive tool designed to help you calculate the area of any space, whether it's a room, yard, or any other area. This calculator supports multiple shapes including rectangles, circles, and triangles, making it perfect for various applications such as flooring projects, painting, landscaping, and construction planning.

The calculator not only provides the area in your chosen unit of measurement but also converts the result to other common units and provides helpful material estimates for common projects like flooring and painting.

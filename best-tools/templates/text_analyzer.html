What is Text Analysis?

Text analysis is the process of examining written content to extract meaningful insights and statistics. Our tool provides comprehensive analysis including word count, readability scores, and keyword density.

This tool is perfect for writers, students, content creators, and anyone who needs to analyze text for length, complexity, or keyword optimization.

Key Features:

  • • Real-time text analysis
  • • Comprehensive word & character counting
  • • Readability scoring
  • • Keyword density analysis
  • • Reading time estimation

Understanding Your Results

Reading Time

Calculated based on average reading speed of 200-250 words per minute. Useful for estimating how long it takes to read your content.

Readability Score

Based on sentence length and word complexity. Lower scores indicate easier reading, while higher scores suggest more complex text.

Keyword Density

Shows how frequently words appear in your text. Useful for SEO optimization and ensuring balanced keyword usage.

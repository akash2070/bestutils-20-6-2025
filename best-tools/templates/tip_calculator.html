ℹ️ About This Tool

Our tip calculator helps you quickly calculate the appropriate tip amount and split bills among multiple people. Whether you're dining at a restaurant, using delivery services, or paying for personal services, this tool ensures you tip fairly and split costs accurately.

Perfect for: Restaurants, cafes, delivery services, hair salons, spas, taxi rides, and any service where tipping is customary.

📝 How to Use

1

Enter Bill Amount

Input the total bill amount before tip in the "Bill Amount" field.

2

Select Tip Percentage

Choose from common tip percentages (15%, 18%, 20%, 25%) or enter a custom percentage.

3

Set Number of People

Enter how many people will split the bill (default is 1 person).

4

Calculate & View Results

Click "Calculate" to see tip amount, total amount, and per-person breakdown.

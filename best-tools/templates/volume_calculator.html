About Volume Calculator

Our volume calculator is a versatile tool designed to help you calculate the volume of various three-dimensional geometric shapes quickly and accurately. Whether you're a student working on geometry problems, an engineer designing components, or someone working on a home improvement project, this calculator provides instant results for the most common 3D shapes.

The calculator supports five fundamental shapes: cube, cylinder, sphere, cone, and rectangular prism. Each calculation uses the standard mathematical formulas and provides results in cubic units based on your input measurements.

How to Use the Volume Calculator

1

Select a Shape: Click on one of the five shape buttons (Cube, Cylinder, Sphere, Cone, or Rectangle) to choose the shape you want to calculate.

2

Enter Measurements: Input the required dimensions in the fields that appear. Different shapes require different measurements (e.g., side length for cube, radius and height for cylinder).

3

Calculate: Click the "Calculate Volume" button to get your result instantly.

4

View Results: The calculated volume will be displayed along with the formula used for the calculation.

Volume Formulas

Cube

V = s³

where s = side length

Cylinder

V = π × r² × h

where r = radius, h = height

Sphere

V = (4/3) × π × r³

where r = radius

Cone

V = (1/3) × π × r² × h

where r = radius, h = height

Rectangular Prism

V = l × w × h

where l = length, w = width, h = height

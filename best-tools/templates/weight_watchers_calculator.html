About Weight Watchers Points Calculator

Our Weight Watchers Points Calculator helps you determine your daily SmartPoints allowance and calculate points for individual foods. This tool uses the official WW SmartPoints formula that considers calories, saturated fat, sugar, and protein content.

The SmartPoints system encourages healthier food choices by assigning higher point values to foods high in calories, saturated fat, and sugar, while rewarding protein-rich foods with lower point values.

Note: This calculator is for educational purposes. For official Weight Watchers coaching and personalized plans, please visit the official WW website.

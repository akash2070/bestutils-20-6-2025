About Z-Score Calculator

A Z-score (also called a standard score) measures how many standard deviations a particular value is away from the population mean. It's a way of standardizing values from different normal distributions so they can be compared directly.

The Z-score formula is: Z = (X - μ) / σ, where X is the raw score, μ is the population mean, and σ is the standard deviation.
